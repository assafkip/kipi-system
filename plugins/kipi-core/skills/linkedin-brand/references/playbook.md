# LinkedIn Playbook (generic reference)

> Framework reference for the linkedin-brand skill. Instance-specific pillars and handles live in `my-project/linkedin-playbook.md`, not here.

## The 2026 algorithm reality

- LinkedIn ranks via **360Brew** — a decoder-only LLM (arxiv 2501.16450). It reads semantic meaning, not keyword matches.
- Organic reach collapsed 66% between Feb 2024 and Sept 2025.
- **Commenting on other people's posts now carries 2.6x the weight of your own posts.** Second-degree comments weigh most.
- **87% of posts die in the first hour.** The first hour is the reach lever, not posting volume.

## Format rules (enforced at draft time)

- 0 hashtags, or 1 high-intent tag max. 3+ tags = -29% reach.
- External links in first comment only. Body links = -32% reach.
- Short sentences, 8-15 words average. One idea per sentence.
- Scar pattern: anchor claims in real experience. Abstract assertions flatten.
- Personal-adjacent beats pure professional. Lead with scar, land on insight.

## Format mix (rolling 30-day window)

| Format | Share | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Text + Image | 40% | +16% engagement Oct 2025 |
| Carousel / Document | 30% | +47% engagement, current winner |
| Article (native long-form) | 15% | +48% reach, rising |
| Text-only | 10% | Still viable if short and scar-anchored |
| Video | 5% | -36% reach since Feb 2025, use sparingly |

## Cadence

- 3 posts per week: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday.
- No daily posting. Posting more hurts underperformers (ranker penalizes low-engagement posts in the feed).
- Reposts count as posts. Include in the 3/week cap.
- Weekend = wildcard slot for niche experiments.
- **70/30 time rule:** 70% time engaging on others' posts, 30% posting your own.
- **Big in big moments:** batch content around launches, funding, product moments. Go quiet between.

## First-hour rule (the actual reach lever)

- 3-5 substantive comments each morning on 2nd-degree target posts (not 1st-degree connections). 2nd-degree comments weigh 2.6x more.
- First comment on your own post within 15 min of publishing. Reply to something real, not self-promo.
- Thread revival: return at hour 2, hour 6, hour 24 and respond to every comment.

## Write for compounding, not the news cycle

- Evergreen topics compound for 2-3 weeks in 360Brew (relevance > recency).
- Commit to 2-3 content pillars. Rotate inside the set.
- Single-topic feels robotic. Pure variety confuses the ranker. Pillars give consistency + range.

## Measurement signals that matter

- Save-to-comment ratio ≥ 0.4 = long-tail reach signal.
- 12-25 unique voices in comments > raw comment count.
- Impressions are vanity. Second-degree comment reach is the real signal.

## Do NOT

- Engagement pods
- Tag unrelated people
- Keyword stuffing
- Auto-cross-post from Twitter/Substack verbatim
- Ask for likes/comments ("drop a fire emoji")
- Comment spam on huge accounts (first-hour target is 2nd-degree, not whales)
- Use em-dashes (AI-detection signal)
- Write rule-of-three lists ("faster, smarter, better" — AI-detection signal)

## Pre-publish checklist

1. Hashtag count: 0 or 1?
2. External links: first comment only, not body?
3. AI phrases scrubbed (see `voice-check.md`)?
4. First sentence scar-anchored? No hedging?
5. Format slot matches current rotation?
6. Day = Tue/Wed/Thu?
7. Plan to land 3-5 2nd-degree comments in the hour after publishing?
